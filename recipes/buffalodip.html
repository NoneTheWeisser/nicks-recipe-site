Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

Get ready to meet the MVP of any party spread! This creamy, spicy, cheesy dip is everything you love about buffalo wingsâ€”minus the mess and plus a whole lot of scoopable goodness. Tender shredded chicken is tossed with tangy buffalo sauce, rich cream cheese, and a gooey blend of cheeses, then baked until bubbly and golden. It's bold, addictive, and perfectly pairs with chips, celery, or just a spoon (no judgment). Warning: once you dip, you won't quit!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 4 ounces crumbled blue cheese
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce, divided
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups cooked shredded chicken

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In large bowl combine cream cheese, sour cream & ranch dressing.
  3. Toss chicken with 1/2 cup buffalo sauce.
  4. Mix in shredded cheddar cheese and crumbled blue cheese.
  5. Spread into the bottom of (8 or 9-inch) deep-dish or small casserole dish.
  6. Bake 15 minutes or until cooked through.

Optional - Slowcooker

  • Add to slowcooker and cook on low for 2-3 hours or high for 1 hours.
